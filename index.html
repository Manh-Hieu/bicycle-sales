Types of Bicycles

Road Bike

Pros: Lightweight, fast, ideal for paved roads.
Cons: Not suitable for rough terrain.

Mountain Bike

Pros: Strong frame, great for off-road and trails.
Cons: Heavier and slower on city roads.

Hybrid Bike

Pros: Comfortable, versatile for city and light trails.
Cons: Not as fast as road bikes or strong as mountain bikes.

Electric Bike (E-Bike)

Pros: Motor support, less effort, good for long distances.
Cons: Expensive and requires charging.
